IT IS NOT JUST A CAMERA

  • Home
  • Blog

Image processing is a method of convert the image to digital form and is a technology of we can make software changes. Image processing can meet the needs of observing hard-to-see objects, identify the image and pattern and search for a high-resolution image.

Smartphones do the same thing in face ID: Image processing. We had to use this technology to fulfil the tasks given in the competition we participated in Robosub. The best way to using this technology was through using a well-qualify camera. When we have bought our camera, we consider features like at least FHD(1080p) resolution and wide lens angle. We were lucky that Lima Bilgisyar sponsored us. We placed this camera below as a navigation camera. This down camera is a key point to completeRobosub path mission. To get full points for this task, the camera must follow the orange path in the mission and the camera must be able to determine the current position of the road by changing its route.

Things are a little complicated for our front camera. First, we started with the Basler Pylon camera for our frontcamera. The front camera's purpose is to recognize the missions and distinguish objects. However, Rasberry Pi camera aimed at taking very quality photos rather than taking videos and also its USB 3.0, transfer and process a little harder and longer. So, we stopped the use Rasberry Pi camera and we ordered another camera which is 100$.
