Planning Our Team

ITU AUV Team has 30 undergraduate student members from different engineering disciplines from Istanbul Technical University. Our team consist of mechanical, electronics, software, and organizational subteams.

Mechanical Team

Designs the chasis, task tools, robotic arm, torpedo,and some other parts, manufactures these components, designing the water tight enclosure for the electronics. To perform such tasks our mechanical team uses Autodesk Fusion 360 as a CAD software.


Mechanical Team Members

Software Team

Designs the algorithms to operate the vehicle along the missions, performing image processing to detect and track objects, controlling the manipulators the interact with the objects around, estimating the position of the vehicle during the mission, builds the autonomy system architecture.

Software Team Members

Organization Team

Manages the financial status of the team, attends to fairs, uses social media to reach people, manages events to increase the popularity of the team. Strengthens communication between sub-teams by organizing in-team activities.

Organization Team Members

Electronics Team

Designs the circuit boards to operate electrical components such as sensors, thrusters and computer, designs the overall connection between electrical components, calculates the power requirements and makes the decisions for suitable hardware.
