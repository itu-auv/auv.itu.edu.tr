Sponsorship Packages

With your sponsorship, our team will be happy with the pride of representing our country in an international highly prestigious competition. Also, your company will be represented by our team among many world famous companies. Sponsorship packages can be met in cash or in the form of technical materials, equipment, transportation, clothing or any other mutually agreed support.

  • Tree planting will be done on behalf of the company.
